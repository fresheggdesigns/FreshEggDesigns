## This project was a research project sponsored by Emory University

What you will find showcased here is a mobile app designed to pair with the physical product design of a Corneal Biomedical Device. The purpose of the mobile app is to create more transparency between technicians & patients, while also preparing patients for the Lasik surgery process.

![](https://fresheggdesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/optrixui_reminders.jpg?w=649)

### Appointments

View Monthly Appointments and set reminders at adjustable interval.

![](https://fresheggdesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/optrixui_messaging.jpg?w=649)

### Messaging

Communication made easy between patients and technicians.

![](https://fresheggdesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/optrixui_appointments.jpg?w=649)

### Checklists

Easily access a user-friendly checklist of key milestones before surgical procedures.
