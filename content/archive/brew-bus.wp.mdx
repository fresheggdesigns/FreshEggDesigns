## Bringing Innovative Technology to the Food Truck Sceen

The Brew Bus project was created for the Georgia Tech Senior Design summit. While the design is conceptual, the research and ideation surrounding this concept is sound, meaning that the Brew Bus could become an actual business model if funded + executed properly.

![](https://fresheggdesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/brewbus-mobileapp_map-screen.jpg)

### Locate

Use the location services of the Brew Bus mobile app to quickly find the bus. See if its at your favorite park or a near by festival!

![](https://fresheggdesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/brewbus-mobileapp_beverage-screen.jpg)

### Pre-Pay (or Not)

The digital payment system of the Brew Bus allows users to either load a Digital card & pay a specific amount for a drink, OR pay for the specific amount of ounces poured in real time.

![](https://fresheggdesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/brewbus-mobileapp_rentals-screen.jpg)

### Reserve

Fill out the form to see about reserving the Brew Bus for your next Corporate Event, Wedding, or Party! Make your next event unforgettable.
